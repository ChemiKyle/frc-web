Welcome to the University of Florida Emerging Pathogens Institute COVID-19 Research Study for Healthy Community First Responders and Emergency Department Employees.

This testing program includes voluntary RESEARCH testing and uses supplies and test kits provided by the University of Florida Emerging Pathogens Institute for which FDA approval is pending. These kits have not yet received FDA or CDC approval, but have been demonstrated to be robust.

This is only for healthy participants. If you are currently sick or exhibiting symptoms please do not register.

The results of your testing will be provided to you via email or phone within 24 to 48 hours. Since this is a research test, please confirm your test results via standard CDC testing as feasible.

If you have any questions regarding the informed consent form, questionnaire, or scheduling, please contact us Monday through Saturday during the hours of 700 to 0000 at 352-733-3999 or EDResearch@health.ufl.edu.
